3D Printed Heart Valves

Created parametrised geometric models of fully functioning 3D printed heart valves. Built corresponding test rig to simulate blood flow and validate performance of the synthatic valve using MRI.

3D PrintHeart is a program at Toronto's Hospital for Sick Children (SickKids) that develops the use of 3D printed heart models to assist doctors in the management of patients with complex congenital heart disease. Hands-On Surgical Training (HOST) courses are also run where surgeons practice various procedures on 3D printed training simulators.

📄 Simulation of semilunar valve function: computer-aided design, 3D printing and flow assessment with MR
SickKids 3D PrintHeart Program

Lunar Rover Powertrain

Design of a full sized concept of a lunar rover powertrain for the 2019 McMaster Designathon: a CAD and design competition hosted at McMaster University. The concept replaced the original Apollo-era rover wheels and steering system by a dust-proof omnidirectional system. The design won first-place in its category.
