PROJECTS

HipPO

Inspired by aquatic therapy, HipPO is a novel hip support system that promotes mobility and relieves pain for those suffering from hip osteoarthritis. The wearable system accomplishes this through adaptive mechanical offloading of body weight, far infrared heat therapy, and compression of the lower limbs. The device is designed to provide a higher quality-of-life to hip OA patients and patients awaiting hip arthroplasty surgeries; for which the backlog has significantly increased due to COVID-19.

CenTo - Centrifugal Toilet

Design of CenTo, a novel centrifugal toilet design for lunar gravity and microgravity. CenTo uses centrifugal force to capture and force human waste through a passive one-way seal mechanism. Also included are a radial fan to manage smell and a specialised seat geometry that cradles the pelvis to help crew align themselves. Manual Neurosurgical Shunt Catheter Tool

A novel manual tool to connect shunt catheters to their valves. Prototypes were designed in Solidworks and 3D printed or machined out of stainless steel. Using this tool shortens the time and number of attempts needed to connect a shunt catheter making it superior to existing techniques. The tool was proven successful and is currently in-use in operating rooms at Toronto's Hospital for Sick Children (SickKids).

Congenital Heart Surgery Simulator

An adjustable dynamic Chest Wall & operating table simulator for 3D PrintHeart: a program at SickKids that develops the use of 3D printed heart models to assist doctors in the management of patients with complex congenital heart disease. The models were used at SickKids' annual Hands-On Surgical Training (HOST) course where surgeons practiced various procedures on teh 3D printed hearts.

Lunar Rover Powertrain

Design of a full sized concept of a lunar rover powertrain for the 2019 McMaster Designathon: a CAD and design competition hosted at McMaster University. The concept replaced the original Apollo-era rover wheels and steering system by a dust-proof omnidirectional system. The design won first-place in its category.
